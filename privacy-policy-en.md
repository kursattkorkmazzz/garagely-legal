# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 16, 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains what data the Garagely application ("App") collects, how it processes that data, and what your rights are. By using the App, you accept this policy.

---

## 1. Data Controller

- **Developer:** Kürşat KORKMAZ
- **Address:** Ankara, Türkiye
- **Contact:** support@garagely.com

This policy has been prepared in accordance with the Republic of Türkiye's **Personal Data Protection Law (KVKK, Law No. 6698)** and, where applicable, the European Union **General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)**.

## 2. Core Principle: Local-First

Garagely is a **local-first** application. This means that the data you enter into the App is stored in your device's local storage and is **not sent** to the Developer's servers.

The Developer **cannot access** any of the following content you enter into the App:

- Vehicle information (brand, model, plate, etc.)
- Expense records and amounts
- Fuel, maintenance, tire, and station records
- Uploaded photos, videos, and documents
- Notes

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Data Collected Automatically Inside the App

**None.** The App does **not** collect analytics, crash reports, usage metrics, or any similar telemetry.

### 3.2 Data You Directly Provide

The following data is stored **only on your device**:

- Vehicle information
- Expense records and financial information
- Maintenance, fuel, tire, and station records
- User preferences (language, theme, currency, time zone, etc.)
- Media added via camera or gallery

### 3.3 Data Processed by the Store (App Store / Play Store)

Subscription and payment transactions are managed by the **Apple App Store** or **Google Play Store**. The privacy policies of these stores apply:

- Apple: https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/
- Google: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Stores may share **aggregated/anonymous** subscription status, purchase history, and billing information with the Developer. The Developer does **not** have access to individual users' payment details.

### 3.4 Support Requests

When you contact us at **support@garagely.com**, your email address and the content of your message are retained for the duration of the support request and are not used for any purpose other than providing support.

## 4. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions. Permissions are used **only with your consent and only for the purpose required by the feature**:

| Permission                 | Purpose                                               | Does data leave the device? |
| -------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- |
| **Camera**                 | Take photos of vehicles, expenses, or documents       | No                          |
| **Photo library**          | Pick existing images/videos/documents from the device | No                          |
| **Notifications** (future) | Reminders for upcoming inspections/maintenance        | No                          |

The App does **not** request **location (GPS), contacts, microphone, health data, or advertising identifiers**.

## 5. Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are launched **only by your action** and Garagely does **not** transmit data to them on your behalf:

- **Map app:** Opens your device's default map app when you tap a station's location
- **Browser:** Opens when you tap a station's website link
- **Phone app:** Opens when you tap a station's phone number

The privacy policies of these third-party services apply in those cases.

## 6. Advertising and Analytics

Garagely shows **no advertisements** and collects **no analytics telemetry**.

## 7. Children's Content

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you become aware that a child under 13 is using the App, please let us know.

## 8. Data Retention

- All data on your device is retained **until you uninstall the App** or replace it via a restore operation.
- Email correspondence for support requests is deleted within **12 months** after the request is resolved.

## 9. User Rights (KVKK Article 11 / GDPR Articles 15-22)

Because all your data is on your device, you can exercise the following rights **independently**:

| Right                               | How to exercise                                                                |
| ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Access**                          | Access all your data directly inside the App                                   |
| **Rectification**                   | Edit the relevant record inside the App                                        |
| **Erasure (right to be forgotten)** | Delete the record or uninstall the App                                         |
| **Data portability**                | Use the Backup feature to export your data as a ZIP file                       |
| **Objection to processing**         | The Developer does not process your data, so no separate objection is required |

For additional questions, you can contact: **support@garagely.com**

Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority: **Personal Data Protection Board (KVKK)** — kvkk.gov.tr

## 10. Data Security

- Your data is stored on your device **with the protection provided by the operating system**.
- Protect your device with PIN/password/biometric lock.
- Ensure your store account is secure during your subscription.
- Keep your backup files (ZIP) in a safe location and consider encrypting them.

## 11. International Data Transfers

Your data resides on your device; **no international transfer takes place**. Only the stores (App Store / Play Store) process subscription and payment data within their own infrastructure — their respective policies apply.

## 12. Changes

If this policy is updated, you will be notified from the related App Store. The "Last updated" date is shown at the top.
